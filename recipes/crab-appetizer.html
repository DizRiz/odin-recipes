King Crab Appetizer


These crab tarts is a simple way to show off your bougie baking skillz

Making tarts is one of the simplest ways to make bite sized food that can feed the house. Top it off with some King Crab and you might just win some extra bougie points to impress your friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 (12 ounce) packages refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (6 ounce) can crab meat, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons thinly sliced green onion
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch paprika

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease 12 tartlet pans.

  2. Step 2

    Divide rolls in half and press into the prepared tartlet pans. Set aside.

  3. Step 3

    In a large bowl, conmbine cream cheese, crab, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Cheddar cheese, green onions, Worcestershire sauce.

    Spoon 1 teaspoon of mixture into tarts and garnish with paprika.

  4. Step 4

    Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 15 to 20 minutes, or until light brown. These freeze wonderfully. Just reheat before serving.
